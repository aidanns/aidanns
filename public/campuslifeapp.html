Campus Life

Campus Life is an iOS application that I developed as part of my final year software engineering project at The University of Melbourne in 2012. You'll be able to find it on the app store starting October 25, 2012.

The application is a crowdsourced directory of places and events of interest to the residents of the university's Parkville campus. It was conceived as a prototype to test the ways in which mobile computing can enhance the on-campus experience for the student body.

If you've tried the app, then I'd love to hear from you at campuslifeapp@gmail.com.
